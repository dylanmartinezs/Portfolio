Tears of Elara

What is Tears of Elara?
Tears of Elara is an open world RPG project, set in a world where magic has changed everything.
My role
During my internships in Orenji games, my main task was to be the game designer for ToE. I made the GDD and coordinated creative decisions. I also helped with the environment building; designed and implemented Inventory, Quests and Dialogue systems; and designed some characters in 3D
